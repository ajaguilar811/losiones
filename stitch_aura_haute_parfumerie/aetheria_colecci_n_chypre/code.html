The tripartite structure of the earth.

Aetheria’s Chypre scents are a study in artisanal contrast. We source our oakmoss from the deep forests of central Europe, pairing it with bergamot from the first pressing in Calabria. This collection represents the 'Gilded Earth'—fragrances that feel as structured as a tailored coat and as organic as the roots of a cedar tree.
